Token Distribution

FluffCoin will have a total supply of 18,446,744,073.709553 tokens.

The distribution of FluffCoin will be as follows:

  • Community Fund: 40% of the total supply
  • Development Fund: 30% of the total supply
  • Liquidity Pool: 20% of the total supply
  • Team and Advisors: 10% of the total supply

FluffCoin aims to create a sustainable ecosystem that incentivizes both users and developers to participate in its growth.
